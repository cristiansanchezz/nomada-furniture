Nómada

Nómada is a set of furniture that is made up of a rest, work and storage element. The purpose of this is to issue a furniture easy to assemble and dissasemble, easy to transport, economical, functional and charged of symbolism. They're designed under the open design philosophy, each furniture have his own instructions downloadable just here! If you have any question, please, contact me!

«It's a conscious and social responsible project, focusing on the material dispossession wich Victor Papanek speaks.»
